Undergraduate Thesis

My Undergraduate thesis was on Free Space Optical Communication. Free-space optical communication system (FSO) is an optical communication technology which uses air as a medium to transmit signal from one place to other via laser beams or non-lasing sources such as LEDs or IREDs, enabling broadband communication where fiber optic cables are unavailable, and line-of-sight can be achieved within a few miles. Our target was to set up a communication link between two buildings of our university campus across 260m,but before that we had to verify all posibilites.
